Weekend Trips

Chennakeshava Temple

Formally known as Sri Lakshmi Chenna Kesava Swamy Temple, it’s located about 5 km from Jadcherla towards Kalwakurthy in Mahabubnagar district.

Sri Meenambaram Shiva Devalayam

One of the ancient Shiva temple situated on the banks of river Dundhubi. This temple is known as Sree Parusha Vedeshwara Swamy Devalayam. This temple is at Meenambaram, Jadcherla, Mahbaoobangar in Telangana State..

Manyamkonda Sri Lakshmi Venkateshwara Swamy Temple

The temple is situated atop Manyamkonda Hill in Mahabubnagar district, Telangana, approximately 16–22 km from Mahabubnagar town, and about 6 km from Devarakadra Railway Station on the Secunderabad–Dronachalam line.

Manyamkonda Hills

Often referred to in context of Manyamkonda Hills, this rocky terrain features steep granite hills, ancient cave shrines, and panoramic views of wooded valleys. The entire area spans three small hills, crowned by the Manyamkonda Temple—an elevated, cave-like shrine of Lord Venkateswara Swamy.
