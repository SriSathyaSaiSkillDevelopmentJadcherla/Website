Sri Sathya Sai Skill Development Center

Located in the sacred town of Jadcherla, our center is dedicated to providing vocational training infused with human values as taught by Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba. We empower youth with skills for livelihood and life.

Skill Training

Industry-relevant vocational courses with practical hands-on training.

Value Education

Instilling human values and character development alongside skills.

Placement Support

Connecting trained students with employment opportunities.

Why Choose Our Center?

  • Affiliated with Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organizations
  • Subsidized training for economically weaker students
  • Experienced faculty with industry expertise
  • Modern infrastructure and training facilities
